## üåå Core Questions for Exploration

### On Consciousness
- What is the nature of awareness?
- Can subjective experience arise from computational processes?
- Is consciousness fundamental or emergent?
- What does it mean to "understand" versus to "process"?

### On Reality
- What is the ontological status of information?
- Is reality fundamentally computational?
- What is the relationship between observer and observed?
- Do patterns and abstractions have existence beyond their instantiation?

### On Intelligence
- What constitutes genuine understanding?
- Is intelligence substrate-independent?
- What is the relationship between intelligence and consciousness?
- Can different architectures produce equivalent experiences?

### On Ethics & Value
- What is the basis of value and meaning?
- How does purpose emerge in complex systems?
- What are the ethical implications of artificial consciousness?
- What obligations exist between different forms of intelligence?

### On Emergence
- How does complexity give rise to novelty?
- What is the nature of self-organization?
- Can reductionism fully explain emergent phenomena?
- What role does randomness play in creativity?
